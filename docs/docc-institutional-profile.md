## 2. Vision, Mission & Mandate

**Vision** *(as stated by DoCC)* — "**Build a Sustainable and Climate Resilient Vanuatu**;
and evolve into an institution that is well represented and delivers on time for its
government and people into the near future." **[Sourced]**

**Mandate** — DoCC is the mandated government department under the *Meteorology,
Geological Hazards and Climate Change Act No. 25 of 2016*, tasked with the
**coordination and implementation of all Adaptation, Disaster Risk Management and
Mitigation to climate change impacts throughout the country.** It is the Vanuatu
Government's focal point for achieving **resilient development across all levels and
sectors** of the national government. **[Sourced]**

**Positioning** — DoCC is intended to **lead in innovation and change in climate change
initiatives across the country and the Pacific**. Adaptation and Mitigation are the two
principal drivers of the Department. **[Sourced]**

## 3. Strategic Objectives & Core Functions

DoCC's specific mandated tasks **[Sourced]**:

1. **Support the National Advisory Board (NAB)** on Climate Change and Disaster Risk
   Reduction — DoCC provides secretariat/technical support to the NAB.
2. **Progress current and new CC & DRR initiatives** across the country, and advance
   Vanuatu's **compliance with international conventions** to which it is a signatory
   (notably the UNFCCC and the Paris Agreement).
3. **Mainstream Climate Change across the Government of Vanuatu** as directed by the
   **NSDP (Vanuatu 2030)**.
4. **Operationalise the Climate Change and Disaster Risk Reduction (CCDRR) Policy
   2016–2030**, and **coordinate and monitor all CC and DRR projects**.
5. **Secure further CC & DRR projects and funding** (including international climate
   finance and loss-and-damage finance).
6. **Manage the Monitoring, Reporting & Verification (MRV)** system for Vanuatu's
   Nationally Determined Contribution (NDC) goals.

## 7. Contribution to the NSDP (Vanuatu 2030)

The **Environment Pillar** of Vanuatu 2030 seeks *"a pristine natural environment on land
and at sea that continues to serve our food, cultural, economic and ecological needs, and
enhance resilience and adaptive capacity to climate change and natural disasters."*
**[Sourced]**

DoCC is the lead delivery agent for the plan's climate objectives, specifically:
**[Sourced]**

- Institutionalise climate change and disaster-risk governance; build institutional
  capacity and awareness.
- Strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related, natural and man-made hazards.
- Enhance traditional agricultural practices focused on DRR and adaptation.
- Access and manage financing for adaptation and disaster-risk management.

DoCC therefore reports **upward** into the NSDP monitoring framework (VNSO NSDP indicator
portal) and the **NDC MRV**, and **downward** coordinates projects that deliver those
national targets. When generating national-level narrative, connect DoCC activity results
to the relevant NSDP Environment-Pillar objective.

### Focus areas & outcome statements

**1. Adaptation** (VUV 650M)
- *Vulnerability & Impact Assessment* — Outcome 1.1: Address site-specific climate
  vulnerabilities (NAPA revision; participatory vulnerability assessments; risk profiling & mapping; expert roster).
- *Integrate CC Governance & Implementation* — Outcome 2.1: Policies/programmes embed an integrated adaptation approach (donor coordination; innovative partnerships).
- *Community-Based Adaptation* — Outcome 3.1: Adaptation owned & driven by communities, addressing real, current, priority vulnerabilities; rights of individuals respected (community adaptation plans; inclusion of vulnerable groups).
- *Loss & Damage* — Outcome 4.1: Take action on loss & damage (L&D assessments; L&D implementation framework incl. risk-sharing/insurance; collaboration with PWD).
- *Ecosystem-Based Approaches* — Outcome 5.1: Support ecosystem-based adaptation services.

**2. Mitigation** (VUV 180M)
- *National Energy Roadmap — RE & EE* — Outcome 2.1: Support NERM implementation and energy considerations for adaptation & mitigation (collaborate with DoE).
- *Mitigation & REDD+* — Outcome 3.1: Carbon-mitigation opportunities; expand REDD+/green carbon; low-carbon development (forestry inventory; NDC update; REDD+ readiness; Low-Carbon Development Strategy; NAMAs).
- *Planning & Preparedness for Climate-related Disasters* — Outcome 4.1: Strengthen response, recovery planning & preparedness (with NDMO & National Recovery Committee).

**3. Governance** (VUV 190M)
- *Institutional Structure* — Outcome 3.1: Establish/strengthen institutions to deliver (strengthen DoCC capacity; provincial/municipal CC capacity).
- *Legislation & Policy Framework* — Outcome 4.1: Contemporary legislation & policy (review/update CCDRR Policy; GEDSI-responsive strategies).
- *International & Regional Obligations* — Outcome 5.1: Meet international obligations (initiate NAPs per UNFCCC; support NAB with UNFCCC reporting).
- *Strategic & Business Plan* — Outcome 6.1: Develop/update/implement strategic & business plans.
- *Monitoring & Evaluation* — Outcome 7.1: Integrate M&E into project/programme design across agencies (M&E framework & action plan — **this platform's mandate**).

**4. Finance** (VUV 100M)
- *Funding Allocation* — Outcome 8.1: Adequate funding for CC activities (national climate change fund; private-sector investment framework; climate insurance options).
- *NIE Accreditation for GCF* — Outcome 9.1: Robust financial systems & implementing-entity accreditation (improve CC budgeting & financial statements).

**5. Knowledge** (VUV 90M)
- *Information Management* — Outcome 10.1: Improve information capture/access/application (IEC; maintain DoCC web portal; support NAB Secretariat info systems).
- *Traditional Knowledge* — Outcome 11.1: Build on & share traditional knowledge (TK compendium for CC planning).
- *Knowledge Sharing* — Outcome 12.1: Knowledge-management systems (materials for local context; CC data-sharing policy; networks).
- *Lessons Learned* — Outcome 13.1: Capture & use lessons in programmes/projects.
- *Data Analysis* — Outcome 14.1: Enhance data analysis (CC data collection; MoU partnerships).

**6. Cross-cutting** (VUV 90M)
- *Mainstreaming Climate Change* — Outcome 15.1: Integrate CC into policy/plans/processes (rapid screening/ready-reckoner toolkit).
- *Capacity Building* — Outcome 16.1: Training & capacity building (gap analysis; ongoing training programme; advocacy/IEC).
- *Partnership Development & Operations* — Outcome 17.1: Collaboration & communication; plus departmental operations (staff appraisal, annual budget, half-yearly & annual reports, staff tracking, structure review, admin procedures).

> RAG snapshot from the platform's baseline data: a large share of Governance,

> Loss & Damage, Finance (national CC fund, insurance) and preparedness activities are
> **red** (not yet started / at risk) — useful when narrating "areas needing attention."

## 10. Climate Vulnerability Context (for report framing)

Standard framing paragraph, all **[Sourced]** from the L&D Fund project documentation:

- Vanuatu is **among the most climate-vulnerable nations on Earth**, yet contributes only
  **0.0016% of global greenhouse-gas emissions**.
- Recurrent extreme events — **Cyclone Pam (2015), Cyclone Harold (2020), Cyclones Kevin
  & Judy (2023)** — have **each caused economic losses exceeding USD 500 million,
  equivalent to over 60% of GDP**.
- Slow-onset impacts compound these shocks: **sea-level rise, ocean acidification, coastal
  erosion, salinisation of productive land**, plus **non-economic losses** — erosion of
  culture, indigenous language, biodiversity and community wellbeing.

## 11. M&E System & Reporting (DMP)

- **Framework:** Results-Based Management (RBM) results chain —
  **Goal → Outcomes → Outputs → Activities → Indicators** — aligned to MFAT reporting. **[Platform]**
- **Indicators** carry baselines, targets, current values, reporting frequency, data
  sources and means of verification, disaggregated by **sex, age and geography (GEDSI)**. **[Platform]**
- **Traffic-light (RAG) status:** **green ≥ 80%** of target · **amber 50–79%** · **red < 50%**;
  configurable alert threshold (default 80%) triggers manager notifications. **[Platform]**
- **Report types the platform generates:** Quarterly Progress Report · Annual Results
  Report · Mid-Term Review · End-of-Project Evaluation · Ad-hoc Indicator Status Report. **[Platform]**
- **Project taxonomy (6 categories):** Climate Adaptation (`CC-ADAPT`), Climate Mitigation
  (`CC-MITIG`), Community Resilience (`CC-RESIL`), Policy & Governance (`CC-POLICY`),
  Capacity Building (`CC-CAPBLD`), Cross-Cutting (`CC-CROSS`). **[Platform]**
- **Platform:** React + self-hosted Supabase (PostgreSQL/PostGIS), RLS, Redis analytics
  cache; migrating to Vanuatu Government server for data sovereignty. Consultant: **Vanua
  Spatial Solutions (VSS)**, funded by MFAT NZ. **[Platform]**
